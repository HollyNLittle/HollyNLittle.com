Welcome to My Blog for Beginner Gardeners

Step #1

Find your zone

I live in Northeast Ohio which is zone 6a-6b. The 1st step I recommend for very beginner gardeners is to use a site like this one; USDA Hardiness Zone Map Look up and take note of your zone, your 1st and last frost dates, remember these dates, you will continiuously refer to this information.

Sites for buying seeds;

Seed Savers Exchange

Unbelievable variety, great information, plus a mission worthy of support.

SeedsNOW.com Botanical Interests

Before buying any of these, I would suggest a visit to your local nursery, one nice thing about them is that you can ask questions and they will have in stock the appropriate seeds for your area at any given time.

These sites are not only good for purchasing seeds, they also provide loads of information to help you make smart decisions regarding what to plant and which varieties to try.
